TPS Varsity Lions Baseball

Our baseball team honors God through athletic excellence, character, and servant leadership. We compete with integrity, respect opponents, and maximize our God-given talents. Baseball offers growth in faith, discipline, and teamwork. Players exemplify Christ's love through sportsmanship, humility, and perseverance. We pursue victory on the scoreboard and in developing young men of faith and character who positively impact their communities.

Values of L.D.R.S.H.I.P.

Our team is guided by seven core values that shape our culture, decisions, and relationships. Together, these values create a resilient, high-performing team culture that delivers exceptional results while supporting the growth and wellbeing of each member.

Loyalty

Standing with your team through challenges and victories.

Discipline

Commitment to daily excellence through consistent effort.

Respect

Honoring teammates, coaches, opponents, and the game itself.

Selflessness

Putting team success above individual achievement.

Honor

Representing our school and families with dignity.

Integrity

Doing what's right even when no one is watching.

Personal Courage

Facing challenges with determination and resilience.
